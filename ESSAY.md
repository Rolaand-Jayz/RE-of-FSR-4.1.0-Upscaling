# Essay — Why This Research Exists

> *"Knowledge is power. Freedom of knowledge is empowerment. Hoarding of knowledge is tyranny."*
>
> — Rolaand Jayz

This page is separate from the technical repository because the research
should be evaluated on its evidence, not on the motivations behind it.

The philosophy is simple: understanding how closed systems work is a
legitimate research activity. Neural network architectures, quantization
schemes, and pipeline structures are knowledge. Publishing what you learn
from studying a binary is not theft of the binary — it is the creation
of new knowledge about how that binary works.

This repository exists to document what FSR 4.1.0 looks like on the
inside, to the extent that static analysis can reveal it. The goal is
not to produce a working replacement. The goal is to make the
architecture understandable, verifiable, and open to scrutiny.

For all technical claims, evidence, and validation status, see the
repository files. This essay is the only place where philosophy appears.
